title: "What is research?"
abstract: >
  What is research? What does a PhD student actually do (other than giving interns work and complaining)? What does a researcher actually do? What are the domains of research investigated by the team? Find out this Thursday! This coffee will be a round table hosted by Romain, Philémon and Théo M. It is intended to be interactive i.e. we will have a very short presentation and will discuss any questions of the audience with potentially different viewpoints! It is mostly aimed towards people joining or aiming to join the world of research.

event: DiverSE Coffee
location: Rennes, France
speaker: Philémon Houdaille, Théo Matricon and Romain Lefeuvre
