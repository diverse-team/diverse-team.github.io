title: "Xareus"
abstract: >
  Xareus is a suite of tools integrated into the Unity3D game engine, designed to accelerate the development of interactive applications, reduce the need for coding and enable domain experts to customise their projects more effectively. At its core lies the scenario engine, a powerful and extensible framework that supports contextual execution and parallelism, enabling for more flexible and efficient applications.
