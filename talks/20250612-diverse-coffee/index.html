!now().past().future().equals(now()): A journey through time, pencils, glue, and heavy metal (silicium)

by Noël Plouzeau
12/06/2025
DiverSE Coffee
Rennes, France

Abstract

The goals of this talk are: (1) to give a glimpse of how CS PhD students/researchers used to work 45 years ago what hardware and software were used how we worked; (2) to recall how the future of this past looked like then and to find out how it differs from our present (time permitting)
